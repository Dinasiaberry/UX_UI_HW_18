About Me

My name is Di’Nasia and I’m a freelance UX/UI designer based in Atlanta, GA. I’m also an artist, product designer and Director of Designs for Collective Minds Inc.

My work spans from web and mobile design and development, graphic design, and user research. I enjoy solving user problems that enhance the accessibility and usability of the product while instilling company branding and identity.

I love working with various technologies including Adobe XD and Xcode, and have worked with various clientele. I bring a psychological and intuitive outlook that conveys a creative problem-solving approach to create desirable and valuable user experiences.

My Career So Far

User Research

With over 5 years of cognitive psychology experience, I’m able to conduct in depth user research through surveys and observation to gain an understanding of human behaviors.

User Empathy

I love integrating my knowledge of human insight to dive deeper into user problems and create personas and further analyses that advocate for user needs based on a true understanding of user issues.

Qualitative Interviews

I love to combine my UX/UI and psychological expertise to create deliberate user interviews and surveys, enabling me to understand the topic and/or product from an authentic source. Doing so allows me to create intuitive designs catered to user pain points.
